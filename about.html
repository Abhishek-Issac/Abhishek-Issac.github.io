My Story

Developer, Builder, Tech Enthusiast

I'm Abhishek Issac, a self-taught developer from Kerala, India. My coding journey began with simple Scratch projects and basic HTML pages, and has evolved into building production-grade Telegram bots that serve thousands of users.

My core expertise is in Python, particularly with Pyrogram and Telethon for Telegram bot development. I've built everything from automated message forwarding systems to full-featured series streaming bots with IMDb integration.

On the database side, I work extensively with MongoDB and PostgreSQL, including complex migration projects and dual-write architectures. I deploy my projects on DigitalOcean Ubuntu VPS servers using Docker.

Beyond the backend, I've ventured into Android development with Kotlin, web development with vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript, and even explored Go for building high-performance bot clients.

When I'm not coding, I'm an avid anime and series watcher, a mobile tech enthusiast who loves tinkering with custom ROMs, and a gamer who enjoys Genshin Impact and Wuthering Waves. I believe technology should be accessible and fun.
